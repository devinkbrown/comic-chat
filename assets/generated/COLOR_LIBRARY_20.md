# Colored launch library inventory

The colored source library covers every one of the 22 bundled legacy avatar
identities. These are project-generated HD source images; they are not a
claim that every identity has already passed the separate six-pose AVB gate.

## Colored old-character coverage

`anna`, `armando`, `bolo`, `cro`, `dan`, `denise`, `hugh`, `jordan`,
`kevin`, `kwensa`, `lance`, `lynnea`, `margaret`, `maynard`, `mike`,
`rebecca`, `sage`, `scotty`, `susan`, `tiki`, `tongtyed`, and `xeno` each
have `avatar-color-hd-v1/<name>-color-hd-v1.png`.

## Twenty colored new-scene sources

The first ten are the colored regular scenes in
`backgrounds-color-hd-v1/tile-00.png` through `tile-09.png`: apartment,
rooftop, cafe, park, space corridor, boardwalk, school hall, rainy street,
library, and campsite.

The next ten are the colored whacky scenes in
`backgrounds-color-hd-v1/whacky-00.png` through `whacky-09.png`: spaceship
bridge, asteroid diner, sky-island market, underwater dome, friendly castle,
pinball interior, cosmic laundromat, cloud train station, mushroom village,
and arcade planetarium.

The corresponding thirty native BGB packages live in `src/assets/generated/`.
They are release candidates until the complete asset provenance manifest and
full application regression gate are signed off.
